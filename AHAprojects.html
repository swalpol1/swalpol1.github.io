American Heart Association Calendar

VICO 1515

This was a project in this typography class to choose a charity you would like to create a calendar for and then let the font be a major point in it. I choose the font Bebas Neue because it is a bold font that can go virtually with any kind of design and make them stand out. I made sure to keep the red throughout the design because the non-profit organization uses red as their brand color. I also included holiday that associated with the organization's goals.
