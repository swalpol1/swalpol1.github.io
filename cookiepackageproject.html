Cookie Package Design

VICO 1515

This was a final project for the typography class. We were given a cookie package template and were told to come up with 3 cookie flavors with a package design each. This also included coming up with the company logo/name, the brand name of cookie, and the flavor of the cookie. I was inspired to use my pug's favorite snacks so the cookie flavors and the logo was inspired by him as well.
